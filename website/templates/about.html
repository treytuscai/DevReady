About DevReady

Learn more about our mission, vision, and how we're empowering junior developers to succeed.

Our Mission

DevReady is an AI-powered coding interview preparation platform created by CS students, for CS students. Our mission is to bridge the gap between theoretical interview problems and real-world coding challenges by providing personalized, interactive coding exercises, real-time AI feedback, and simulated mock interviews.

What We Offer

  • Personalized Challenges: Tailored coding problems that adapt to your progress and target your areas for improvement.
  • Interactive Code Interpreter: Write, run, and test your code with instant feedback from our integrated interpreter.
  • AI-Powered Assistance: Leverage our GPT-4o Mini AI Helper for guidance based on your current code and problem context.
  • Comprehensive Problem Library: Access a diverse set of coding challenges organized by topics, difficulty, and concept.

Our Vision

We believe that every junior developer deserves the opportunity to excel. By focusing on practical, step-by-step problem solving and realistic coding challenges, DevReady aims to empower you to tackle even the most complex interview questions and kickstart a successful tech career.
